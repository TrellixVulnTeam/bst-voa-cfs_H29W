How do I correct and submit reports with data validation failures?

Reports with data validation failures cannot be submitted. You must either correct the data issues that have caused the failure; or remove the reports that have failed.

To correct data issues, you can:

  • Edit individual reports. Click the ‘Edit’ link in the row of a report with data issues, locate the highlighted errors and correct them.
  • Delete the uploaded file, correct the reports, and re-upload the file. Click the button ‘Delete all reports’ at the bottom of the table. Confirm your action on the confirmation page

To remove reports with data validation failures, you can:

  • Remove individual reports. Click the ‘Remove’ link in the row of a report with data issues. Confirm your action on the confirmation page.
  • Remove all reports with data issues. Click the button ‘Remove failed reports only’ at the bottom of the table. Confirm your action on the confirmation page.

Once all data issues have been corrected or removed, a button should appear allowing you to submit your reports to the VOA.”
